Drabyam Pain Relief Oil

Description

Drabyam Pain Relief Oil uses an effective formulation designed to provide safe and fast relief from arthritis, stiffness, gout, muscular pain, joint pain, back pain, and frozen shoulder.

Ingredients:

Wintergreen Oil - 10%
Menthol - 6%
Eucalyptus Oil - 4%
Camphor- 5%
Turpentine Oil - 4%
Base- 71%

WARNINGS:


Not to be used in infants under 2 years. It is dangerous to put camphor-containing products into the nostrils of infants.

DIRECTIONS: FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY

1.  Use 4-5 drops of the oil on the affected part of the body and rub gently.

2.   Repeat as necessary.

PRECAUTIONS

1.   Protect your eyes while using this product. Do not rub your eyes after handling the oil.

2.  For sensitive skin and allergies, it is advisable to patch test using a small drop and discontinue if irritation or burning sensation occurs.

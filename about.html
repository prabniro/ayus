Ayus Herbal Care Pvt. Ltd. is a Nepal-based company that specializes in manufacturing natural wellness and healthcare products. Established in 2013, our head office is located in Saumil Complex, Sinamangal, Kathmandu – 9, Nepal, while our factory is situated at V.D.C. Vodaha, Bara – 6, Nepal. We take pride in using only the highest quality ingredients in our products, with a focus on sustainability and organic practices. Our mission is to provide safe, effective, and affordable natural remedies to promote wellness and improve overall health.

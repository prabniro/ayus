Drabyam liquid Cold Bam

Description

Drabyam Pain Relief Oil uses an effective formulation designed to provide safe and fast relief from arthritis, stiffness, gout, muscular pain, joint pain, back pain, and frozen shoulder.

Ingredients:

Wintergreen Oil - 10%
Menthol - 6%
Eucalyptus Oil - 4%
Camphor- 5%
Turpentine Oil - 4%
Base- 71%

WARNINGS:


Not to be used in infants under 2 years. It is dangerous to put camphor-containing products into the nostrils of infants.

DIRECTIONS: FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY

1.   For common cold and cough, headache, and nasal congestion, put a few drops of balm in warm water and inhale its steam vapor. For dry inhalation, put a few drops in a handkerchief and inhale the aroma.

2.   Massage liquid cold balm onto the chest and throat as a decongestant.

3.   For muscle and joint pain take a few drops of liquid cold balm and rub it onto the affected part 2-3 times a day.

PRECAUTIONS

1.   This preparation should not be used on infants and pregnant women.

2.  Avoid contact with eyes, ears, and genital parts.

3.  Do not use it on open damaged skin or on open wounds.

4.  Stop use and consult your doctor if excessive irritation and rashes develop in the skin.

Yashima Mosquito repellent Roll on

Description 1

Yashima Mosquito repellent Roll on provides Long-lasting protection from all kinds of mosquitoes, flies and other insects. Safeguard from diseases transmitted by Mosquitoes like dengue, Malaria, and Japanese Encephalitis.

Ingredients:

Citronella (Cymbopogon flexuosus) Oil– 10%
Menthol – 1%
Eucalyptus Oil – 2%
Base – 87%

WARNINGS:


Not to be used in infants under 2 years. It is dangerous to put camphor-containing products into the nostrils of infants.

DIRECTIONS: FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY

1.  Roll gently onto the exposed skin, except the face.

2.   Repeat if required.

PRECAUTIONS

1.   If pregnant and have a medical condition, consult a health professional before use

2.  Avoid contact with eyes, ears, and genital parts.

3.  Do not apply on cuts and wounds or irritated skin

4.  Do not use it in case of skin allergies.
